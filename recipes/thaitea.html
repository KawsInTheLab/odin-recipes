Thai Tea

Description

Thai tea is a sweetened drink that originated from Thailand and popularized there as well. Thai tea is easily made using thai tea mix/leaves, sugar, water, half and half, and condensed milk.

Ingredients

  • water
  • thai tea mix/leaves
  • cane sugar
  • half and half
  • condensed milk

Steps

  1. First, you need to measure about 8 cups of water and pour it into your pot.
  2. Second, boil the pot of water until bubbling.
  3. Next, pour your 1 1/2 cups of thai tea mix as well as your 1 1/4 sugar into the boiling water.
  4. Immediately, turn down heat to low-medium and begin stirring for 3 minutes.
  5. After 3 minutes, turn off heat and let your tea cool down for 20-25 minutes.
  6. Next, pour your tea through a strainer into a pitcher, then let it cool in refrigerator for 2-3 hours before serving.
  7. When serving, pour your tea about halfway of 2/5 of the glass and then use your half and half for another 2/5 of the glass. Mix and taste, and add condensed milk as you see fit to get that sweet or not so sweet taste depending on your preferences.
