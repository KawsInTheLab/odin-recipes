Spicy Noodles

Description

Korean Spicy Noodles are a quick ramen snack from the Buldak company.

Ingredients

  • water
  • ramen
  • seaweed
  • sesame seeds
  • spicy sauce

Steps

  1. First, you need to measure about 4-5 cups of water and pour it into your pot.
  2. Second, boil the pot of water until bubbling.
  3. Next, put your block of ramen into the boiling water.
  4. Immediately, turn down heat to low-medium and close lid for about 3 minutes.
  5. After 3 minutes, turn off heat and strain out as much water as possible.
  6. Next, pour your noodles into a bowl and add your seaweed, sesame seeds, and spicy sauce to your noodles.
  7. Mix the ingredients well and enjoy! (Always feel free to add your own touches, like eggs, meat, etc.)
